Getting Started with I2P Browsers

December 20, 2025
Home Blog

To browse I2P sites (eepsites), configure your browser to use I2P's local proxy:

  • HTTP Proxy: 127.0.0.1:4444
  • SSL Proxy: 127.0.0.1:4445

After setup, visit any .i2p address like zzz.i2p!

Tip: Use Firefox with strict privacy settings for best results.

Use at your own risk.
